          <h4>Set ROI Goals & Divide the Work</h4>

          <p style="margin-bottom:10px;"><strong>ROI Planning —</strong> SueJean and the segment lead align on the event's ROI target, working backwards from the cost of attendance to determine how many deals need to close to justify the investment.</p>
